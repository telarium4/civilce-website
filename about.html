About CivilCE

Your Trusted Partner in Professional Engineering Education

Our Mission

To provide Professional Engineers with high-quality, accessible, and affordable continuing education that advances their careers while ensuring public safety through competent engineering practice.

Who We Are

CivilCE is a leading provider of Professional Development Hours (PDH) for licensed Professional Engineers across the United States. Founded in 2024, we specialize in delivering quality continuing education courses that meet state requirements while providing practical, real-world knowledge.

Our comprehensive catalog covers all major engineering disciplines, from structural and transportation to water resources and environmental engineering. Each course is developed by experienced PEs who understand the challenges and opportunities in today's engineering landscape.

We're proud to serve engineers in Nevada and 40+ other states, helping thousands of professionals maintain their licenses while advancing their expertise.
